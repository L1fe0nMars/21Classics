1971 Oldsmobile 442

An absolute survivor 442, this is one of the finest and most original examples to be found anywhere. You are looking at an original paint car with all its original components in exceptional condition. Power is derived by its matching numbers big block 455 cubic inch motor topped off with its original four barrel carburetor. Many options on this car including factory air conditioning, vinyl roof, buckets and console, optional steering wheel, SSII rims, tinted glass, AM/FM stereo, and more. This car has only been driven 38,500 miles since new. Original build sheet included with its presentation.
