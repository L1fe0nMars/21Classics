-1955 Ford Sunliner Convertible-

With a striking combination factory paint scheme, many enthusiasts dating back to the 1950s called this paint scheme Bumblebee. Powered by the original 272/V8 powerplant matted to an automatic transmission. Notice the heads up style display speedometer. Other features include a factory power top, original radio, deluxe wheel covers, beautiful stainless steel trim, tinted glass, factory continental kit, and wide whitewall bias ply tires. A very comfortable cruiser with style, grace, and lots of flash, this is 1950s all the way.
